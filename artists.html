Meet the Artists

Mark Moreau

Mark Moreau is a founding member and lead base player of Magsaysay. Mark a veteran army major, a husband and father of three children. He is also certified personal trainer and a certified instructor of Zumba, CrossFit, and Body Building.

Kevin Nomak

Kevin Nomak founded Magsaysay with Mark Moreau in an old community town hall. Kevin is a guitarist, bass player, and vocalist. He is also a martial arts instructor. He has won many awards and recognition for his contribution to the music industry.

Robert Bandana

Robert Bandana is an award-winning drummer, singer, and songwriter. He has been with Magsaysay since its inception. Robert is a former professional boxer and a former professional wrestler. Robert has collaborated with many big names in the music industry and has been featured in many hit music productions.

Emma Brown

Emma Brown is a professional dancer and choreographer, and a vocalist. She has been known as the queen of the dance floor. Emma is also a solo artist with many hit songs, grammy awards, and accolades. Emma is also a U.S. Marines veteran and a former professional wrestler.

Trevor Smith

Trevor Smith is Magsaysay's leading vocalist and songwriter. He has been with Magsaysay since its inception. Trevor has been featured on many hit music productions and has collaborated with many big names in the music industry. He is also the band's producer and recording engineer.
